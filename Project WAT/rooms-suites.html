Stay Ease Rooms & Suites

Rooms:

Standard Rooms

  • Size: 200-300 sq. ft.

  • Bedding: One queen/king bed or two twin beds.
  • Features: Basic view, simple decor
  • Amenities:
    • Free Wi-Fi
    • Flat-screen TV
    • Air conditioning
    • Desk, coffee/tea maker
    • Basic toiletries

Deluxe Rooms

  • Size: 300-400 sq. ft.

  • Bedding: One king bed or two double/queen beds.
  • Features: Enhanced views, stylish decor
  • Amenities:
    • All standard room amenities
    • Mini-bar
    • In-room safe
    • Bathrobe and slippers
    • Enhanced toiletries

Executive Rooms

  • Size: 350-450 sq. ft.

  • Bedding: One king bed or two queen beds.
  • Features: Business-friendly furnishings, better views
  • Amenities:
    • All deluxe room amenities
    • Large work desk, ergonomic chair
    • Access to executive lounge
    • Complimentary breakfast, newspaper
    • Turndown service

Suites:

Junior Suites

  • Size: 400-600 sq. ft.

  • Bedding: One king bed
  • Features: Separate sitting area, elegant decor
  • Amenities:
    • All executive room amenities
    • Separate sitting area with sofa
    • Two flat-screen TVs
    • Personalized check-in/check-out

Full Suites

  • Size: 600-1,200 sq. ft.

  • Bedding: One king bed
  • Features: Separate living and sleeping areas, luxurious decor
  • Amenities:
    • All junior suite amenities
    • Dining table, larger living room
    • Fully equipped kitchen (some suites)
    • Multiple flat-screen TVs
